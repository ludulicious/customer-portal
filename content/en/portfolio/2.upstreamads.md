# UpstreamAds.com - Advanced Advertising Analytics

![UpstreamAds website](/images/portfolio/upstreamads/main.png){.rounded-lg height="600" width="1000"}

UpstreamAds.com is a comprehensive advertising platform designed to help businesses maximize their return on investment through intelligent campaign optimization and detailed analytics.

## Key Features

- **Campaign Management**: Create, monitor, and optimize advertising campaigns across multiple platforms
- **Advanced Analytics**: Deep insights into campaign performance 
- **Real-time Reporting**: Live performance metrics and automated alerts
- **Scheduled and on demand reports**: Automated emails with progress info or on demand


## Impact

UpstreamAds.com has helped businesses increase their advertising ROI by an average of 35% through intelligent optimization and detailed analytics. The platform processes over 1 million ad impressions daily.

![UpstreamAds portal](/images/portfolio/upstreamads/1-dark.png){.rounded-lg height="600" width="1000"}

## Technologies Used

- **Frontend**: VueJs, Typescript, Vuetify
- **Backend**: Asp.Net core, .Net 9, C#
- **Database**: PostgreSQL with ServiceStack OrmLite
- **Caching**: Redis for high-performance data caching
